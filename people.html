Tatiana Capitanio

Founder of Data4Good, Tatiana Capitanio has always been passionate about dedicating herself to social issues and using her skills for the greater good. That's why Data4Good came to her mind as a way of using her passion and skill with data for social good.

Graduated in Marketing with a master in Business Administration, Tatiana has more than 8 years of experience in marketing, sales, communication and corporate social responsibility in big companies. She has worked for more than 4 years in Google Brazil, developing industry analysis for sales. Within Google she has dedicated part of her time to lead and promote volunteering activities in Latin America.

Tatiana is also an One Young World Ambassador for Brazil. 
Music, yoga and travel are her passions, and she believes that empathy is the key for positive changes in the world. :)

Filippe Barros

Filippe is graduated in Marketing from ESPM-SP, post graduated in Strategic Management by FIA and is a master's candidate in Management at FEA-USP, researching about strategies for NGOs and social business.

Filippe began his career on marketing in the pharmaceutical industry. Later he worked in a consultancy for planning and implementing sponsorship strategy and for consulting and fundraising for projects and institutions. Currently, besides cofounder of Data4Good, Filippe Barros is a speaker, thesis advisor at FIA University Specialisation course, and professor at Faculdade Sumaré.
